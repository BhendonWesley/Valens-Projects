Valens Projects ("Valens Projects," "we," "us," or "our") provides carpentry, framing, roofing, siding, deck, addition, outdoor living, interior fine finish and snow removal services across Massachusetts, Connecticut, Maine, New Hampshire and Rhode Island. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through valensprojects.com (the "Site"), how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to give us directly. The Site's "Request a Free Estimate" contact form asks for:

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or advertising trackers on this Site. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Respond to your request for an estimate or quote

  • Keep records related to any project we complete for you

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share your information with subcontractors, suppliers, or service providers directly involved in delivering the work you've requested, or when required to do so by law.

Data Retention

We keep the information you submit for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and, if you become a customer, for as long as necessary to complete your project and satisfy our warranty, tax, and legal record-keeping obligations.

Your Choices & Rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us using the details below. We will respond to reasonable requests as promptly as we can.
