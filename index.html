Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does Valens Projects serve? expand_more

We serve homeowners across Massachusetts, Connecticut, Maine, New Hampshire and Rhode Island. Our crews travel to projects throughout all five states, so wherever you are in our service area you get the same in-house team and standard of work.

What services do you offer? expand_more

Framing, roofing, siding, decks, additions, outdoor living spaces, interior fine finishes and seasonal snow services — all handled by one in-house team, so you're not juggling separate contractors for each trade.

Are you licensed and insured? expand_more

Yes — Valens Projects is licensed and insured to operate in each state we serve. Ask us for our license and insurance details when you request your estimate.

Do you offer a warranty on your work? expand_more

Every project includes a 1-year warranty, in writing, with quality checks at every phase of the build — not just a walkthrough at the end.

Is your pricing transparent, and how do I get a quote? expand_more

You'll know the number before we start — no surprise line items. Reach out via the form above, call, or message us on WhatsApp with your project details and we'll put together a free estimate.

Do you subcontract the work, or is it all in-house? expand_more

100% in-house. The same team that frames your project also handles the roofing, siding and finish work — no revolving door of subcontractors.
